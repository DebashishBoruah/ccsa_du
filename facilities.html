RESEARCH

The Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D) programme covers Computer Science

Eligibility for Admission :
1. A candidate (other than candidates from Medical and Engineering streams) seeking admission to the course for the degree of Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) shall have to obtain at least 50% marks in Master's Degree OR equivalent grade point.All applicants seeking admission to the doctoral programme of the University [except foreign students applying for admission as provided for under clause 9] must clear the Dibrugarh University Research Admission Test (DURAT) Candidates with M.Phil degree (regular mode) are exempted from appearing in the DURAT.
2. A candidate may also be enrolled if he /she has any other degree recognised as equivalent to the Master’s Degree in the subject in which the candidate wishes to pursue a course of research, provided the candidate fulfills the conditions at (i) above.
3. Under special circumstances, a candidate may be allowed by the Research Council to undertake research in a subject other than one in which he/ she has obtained Master’s Degree. Before permitting the candidate to do so, the Council shall satisfy itself that the branches are inter-related and /or the candidate is competent to undertake research in the particular subject as evidenced by published/practical works.
4. For doing Ph.D. in Engineering/Technology/Medical Science, a candidate must have a Masters’ Degree in Engineering / Technology/Medical Science with a minimum of 55% marks.
5. A candidate with a B.E./B.Tech Degree with a valid JRF-GATE score may also be considered for admission to the Ph.D. programme.

Admission Procedure:
1. The candidate shall apply for admission to the Chairperson, Departmental Research Committee of the Department in which he/she desires to pursue research.
2. The application for admission must be made in the prescribed form obtainable from the Office of the Dy. Registrar, (Academic) on payment of prescribed fees and will be received throughout the year. The application must be submitted through a recognised Ph.D. Supervisor and should be accompanied by an outline of statement in triplicate on the subject of the proposed investigation.

Note : For other regulations, please consult Dibrugarh University Regulations for the Research Council and for the Degree of Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) 2015
